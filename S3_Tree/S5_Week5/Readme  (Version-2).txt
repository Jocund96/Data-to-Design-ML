2. UHPC-Dataset.xlsx = Main dataset in MS Excel format, containing 2,188 UHPC mix designs from 168 research articles. The “UHPC Dataset” tab compiles complete mixture compositions, curing regimes, workability, mechanical, and durability properties, with each row linked to its original publication using a unique research paper numbering system. All other tabs provide supporting data on cements composition, SCMs composition, nano additives composition, fillers composition, and related testing standards references. Also "References and Data source" gives the references and DOI link for the publications. 

3. Summary of the UHPC Dataset: This file summarizes the structure and content of the global UHPC mix design database. This summary file is designed to help users quickly understand the dataset scope, material coverage, and the types of properties and mix designs included.
Each tab provides outlined details:
	Regional and Year Wise Publication: Lists the number of UHPC mix designs and source publications by country and year. 
        Cement and Composition: Summarizes the types and chemical composition of cement used in the dataset. 
        SCMs and Nano Particles: Details the supplementary cementitious materials (SCMs), nano additives, and their chemical compositions found across the dataset. 
        Sand and Fillers: Lists the types of sand (e.g., quartz, river, silica) and sustainable fillers (e.g., glass powder, recycled brick, iron tailings) used, as well as their composition. 
        Fibers: Details the types of fibers (metallic, synthetic, mineral, hybrid), their geometric and mechanical properties (e.g., length, diameter, tensile strength), and summarizes their usage across the database.
        Superplasticizer: Provides information on the different types of superplasticizers used in UHPC mixes. 
        Curing Regime: Provides information on the curing methods (standard, steam, heat, autoclave, etc.), including mean and range of temperature, humidity and pressure.
        Specimens and Properties: Summarizes testing parameters and results, such as workability (slump, slump flow), mechanical properties (compressive, tensile, and flexural strengths), and durability (shrinkage, chloride permeability, freeze-thaw resistance, etc.). It also lists specimen sizes and types used in these tests.
        Testing Standards: Lists all the testing standards referenced across the dataset. The standards cover testing of compressive strength, flexural strength, splitting tensile strength, durability (e.g., shrinkage, chloride permeability, freeze–thaw resistance), and other performance properties. 
        Distribution of 28 Days Compressive Strength: Presents the statistical distribution and overall trends for 28-day compressive strength values in the database.

4. Description: This file offers an overview of the UHPC dataset’s purpose and scope. It summarizes the dataset content and its value for data-driven research and model development.
